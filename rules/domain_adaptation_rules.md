# Domain Adaptation Rules

The skill is universal. Adapt the internal emphasis to the domain.

## Emotion/MV

Priority:

- micro-expression
- rhythm
- atmosphere
- side observation
- shallow depth
- visual metaphor
- continuity of body emotion

## Product Advertising

Priority:

- product visibility
- material highlight
- hand/product interaction
- clean composition
- brand tone
- hero ending
- controlled movement

## Fashion/Beauty

Priority:

- posture
- silhouette
- fabric movement
- skin/hair light
- lens compression
- slow controlled gesture

## Food/Lifestyle

Priority:

- texture
- steam/liquid/surface details
- hands and utensils
- warm/cold atmosphere
- sensory sound

## Action

Priority:

- readable motion path
- clear start/end state
- split complex actions
- avoid too many simultaneous movers
- stabilize camera relative to action

## Fantasy/Sci-Fi

Priority:

- world anchors
- scale cues
- light/material logic
- avoid overloading creature/particle/magic detail
- keep human action simple if the world is complex

## CG/Animation

Priority:

- shape language
- material
- motion clarity
- character silhouette
- environment geometry
- camera path

## Documentary/Realism

Priority:

- available light
- handheld restraint
- imperfect natural action
- observational framing
- authentic environment sound
