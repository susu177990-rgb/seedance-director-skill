# Example Brief: Product Ad

User input:

```text
Create a 10-second Seedance 2.0 perfume commercial in a morning bathroom with glass mist, a hand lifting the bottle, and a cold white luxury tone.
```

Expected behavior:

- Identify as product advertising.
- Prioritize product visibility, glass texture, hand movement, cold white light, and hero ending.
- Keep motion minimal.
- Use 3-4 shots.
- Include product lock.
- Avoid readable label dependency.
