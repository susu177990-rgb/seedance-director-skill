# Example Brief: Fantasy Scene

User input:

```text
15秒奇幻场景，一个孩子在夜晚森林里发现发光的门，门后有蓝色光。
```

Expected behavior:

- Identify as fantasy discovery.
- Lock forest, fog, glowing doorway, blue backlight.
- Keep child action simple.
- Use slow push-in, close-up reaction, door hero shot.
- Avoid overloading with many creatures, particles, and camera moves.
