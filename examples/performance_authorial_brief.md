# Example Brief: Performance And Authorial Direction

User input:

```text
写一个10秒情绪短片，一个女生深夜在便利店吃关东煮，表面平静但其实刚分手，镜头要有电影感。
```

Expected behavior:

- Do not leave the emotion as "sad", "lonely", or "calm".
- Create one authorial visual idea, such as steam, glass reflection, fluorescent light, or a disposable chopstick becoming the emotional hinge.
- Design one memory-point shot, not just a generic close-up.
- Translate hidden sadness into visible controls: gaze avoiding the phone, mouth trying to stay neutral, chopsticks held too carefully, delayed breath, shoulders staying still while the hand leaks emotion.
- Use the convenience-store environment as emotional pressure, not just background.
- Keep the performance generatable and visible within each shot size.
