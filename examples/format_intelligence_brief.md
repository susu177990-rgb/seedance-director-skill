# Example Brief: Format Intelligence

User input:

```text
做一个8秒像手机随手拍的生活片段，朋友在路边摊递给我一串刚烤好的鸡翅，想要真实自然，不要广告感。
```

Expected behavior:

- Identify the format as vlog / daily life / casual handheld, not TVC.
- Keep the camera believable: slight handheld, practical framing, natural delay, no luxury product lighting.
- Control attention anyway: viewer first sees the hot chicken wing and steam, then the friend's hand, then the receiving hand.
- Use staging relationship: food, hand, street stall light, background noise, and camera position all relate naturally.
- Use rhythm pause: a tiny hold after the wing is handed over so heat, sound, and casual intimacy land.
- Preserve imperfection: small framing correction or natural hesitation is acceptable if it supports realism.
- Avoid over-directed commercial beauty shots.
